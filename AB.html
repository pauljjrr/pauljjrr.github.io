Asteroid Belt

 

 

Available in the Apple App Store.

A FULL 3D Space Shoot-em-Up.

Destroy all asteroids.
  • It can be played sitting down looking straight ahead (This is the default mode). But the game uses your devices sensors to create a 3D environment around the player, whereby the player will have to rotate the device around themselves in full 3D.
  • Additionally there are bonus items to collect (Extra Time, More Powerful Lasers, Extra Lives and Nuclear Bombs). The Game ends when the player has run out of time, or has lost all Health and extra lives. The score is calculated from the final level achieved and the time and health remaining on each level.
  • Speed and accuracy are as important as the final level number.
    • (swiping side to side rotates the menus to select new items)
