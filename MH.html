My Hours

 

 

 

 

 

 

Simple little app to help calculate your work hours.

Choose a day,

Enter Start Time,

Enter Finish Time,

Rinse and repeat.

 

Total hours are displayed for each day and the full week.

 

Settings are available on the help screen to adjust 12hr/24hr clock and the time increments on the time pickers. All entered data is saved to help speed up susequent uses of the App.
